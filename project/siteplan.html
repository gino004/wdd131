Project Site Plan

Site Name:

The name intended for this project will be called Estrella Travel Agency.

Domain Name

www.EstrellaTravelAgency.com

Site Purpose:

The site on this page will provide services to customers with spectacular discounts for their trips and every month there will be an event for children and adults so they can learn more about the company and there will be prizes for the best premium customers.

Scenarios:

  • What are the best prices to have an efficient and safe flight?
  • What are the most popular destinations for this season?
  • What destinations do you recommend for a family vacation/honeymoon/adventure trip?
  • Do you offer complete tour packages?
  • What does the package include (flights, accommodation, tours, meals, transportation)?

Color Schema:

The darker colors will be used to place it in the header and footer, then the lighter colors will be placed as the background in the main and the lighter colors will be used for small cards like a weather event or an event.

Typography:

Two fonts will be used for this page which are "Signika Negative" and "Nunito Sans", the first will be used to place the header and footer and the other for the rest of the page.
